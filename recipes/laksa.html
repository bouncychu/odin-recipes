Laksa Recipe


A delicious bowl of laksa


About Laksa

Laksa is a Singaporean delicacy that consists of noodles in a thick coconut gravy.

It is often spicy and contains flavours of the ocean such as prawns and cockles.

Ingredients:

  • Thick rice noodles
  • Coconut milk
  • Laksa Paste
  • Cockles
  • Fish Cake
  • Curry Leaves
  • Prawns

Steps for preparation:

  1. Stir fry the cockles, curry leaves, fish cake & prawns in oil.
  2. Add the laksa paste and fry until fragrant.
  3. Introduce the coconut milk to the mix and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Cook the rice noodles until firm.
  5. Serve while hot.
