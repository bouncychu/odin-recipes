Chicken Rice Recipe


An irresitable plate of chicken rice


About Chicken Rice

Chicken Rice is a Singaporean delicacy that consists of chicken and rice.

It is often served with chili, ginger and black soy sauces and come in roasted or the more traditioanl steamed version.

This recipe will focus on the "steamed" version of chicken rice.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Pandan leaves
  • Whole Chicken
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black soy sauce
  • Ice
  • Chillies

Steps for preparation:

  1. Boil the chicken in water for 20 minutes or until cooked through
  2. Cool it imediately in a bowl of ice.
  3. Use the chicken stock made from boiling the chicken to cook rice in rice cooker with pandan leaves.
  4. Blend ginger, garlic and chilli to make a sauce, be sure to add salt to taste.
  5. Carve the chicken, plate it on top of the rice with the sauce on the side.
