Cheese Sandwhich Recipe


A perfect cheese sandwhich.


About this Cheese Sandwhich

The cheese sandwhich needs no introduction.

Everybody loves cheese in between 2 pieces of bread so let's jump into it

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Mayo
  • American cheese

Steps for preparation:

  1. Spread Mayo on the top and bottom pieces of bread.
  2. Layer on the sharp cheddar cheese.
  3. Add as much American cheese as you would like.
  4. Cook in air fryer for 5 minutes at 200 Celcius
